Mill Rolls

Secure mill rolls with heavy-duty chains or wire rope slings, using appropriate edge protection and properly tensioned lashings.

Breakbulk

Lash breakbulk cargo with a combination of chains, wire rope slings, and web lashings, ensuring proper load distribution and securing points.

Project Steel

Lash steel project cargo with heavy-duty chains or wire rope slings, using appropriate edge protection and properly tensioned lashings, while considering the center of gravity and load distribution.
